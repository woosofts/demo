About Us

Excellence In True Sense

Majority of students aspire to become ‘Doctor & Engineer’ but very few of them realize their aspirations because they lack proper guidance despite deserving to compete. In fact, if they are motivated and trained properly for the preparation of these competitive exams with an edge, they are bound to succeed. Aiming to help realize the dreams of thousands of such students, Excellence Institute was founded in the year 1988. It was started as a humble coaching institute with 12 students only, who trusted on our simple vision of enlightening the deserving minds. Gradually, with the consistent support of well-wishers and mentors, Excellence Institute has today become a rare phenomenon.
