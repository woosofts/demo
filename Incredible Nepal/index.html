Trekking & Expedition

Expedition in Nepal is famous & is meant for adventure lovers. Expeditions offers choices to go for expedition in 8 of the world’s highest peak among 14 peaks having an altitude over 8000m. Significantly, Nepal is famous for holding Mt. Everest (8848m) which is the tallest mountain of the world.

Water Rafting

At the foot of the Himalayan Mountains, Nepal is blessed with an amazing diversity of rivers. Simply put, we live in a white water enthusiast’s paradise with an awesome variety of white water rafting adventures to tempt any type of traveler.

Paragliding

Paragliding is one of the most exciting adventures. It is recently growing popularity in Nepal. Nepal is the second choice after Switzerland for paragliding. Pokhara is main paragliding station in Nepal.
